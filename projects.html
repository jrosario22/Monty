NYC Math Scores Data

This project looked at New York City Math exam scores of students with and without disabilities from 2006 - 2012. Using statistical inferences and R, I was able to analyze the data to answer questions such as:

  • Do students with disabilties struggle more with math compared to students who do not have a disability?
  • Do students who succeed in math continue to do so as they progress?
  • Are scores consistent throughout the grade levels?
  • Are scores consistent throughtout the year?

Set Game

The Set game is a math card game. Three cards make up a "set", which a player collects by making sure each of those three cards has exactly the same matching feature or else all have a different feature. If two cards match in one of the four features, but the third card does not, then the three cards are not a "set" and the player is penalized instead of being able to collect. Made with Xcode in Swift.
